#  Dolomiti Vintage Eyeglasses

"Dolomiti VIntage" is a collection of new, never used, perfect condition
eyeglasses made in the 1960's to 1990's. All products are carefully selected
for their quality components, styling, attention to detail, and were all hand
made in European factories, mostly Italy, Germany, or France. These frames we
have procured from the "vault", archived products that have not seen the light
of day since they were made 40 to 60 years ago. Nowadys, "classic" eyewear
steals design tips from these original  vintage glasses,  and the new
manufactured eyeglasses are mostly made in China from low quality components.
Dolomiti VIntage glasses are the original, true source - wouldn't you rather
own the real McCoy versus a cheap knock off?
