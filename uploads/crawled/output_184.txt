Safilo Elasta Eyewear is one of the world's largest makers of eyeglasses and
sunglasses. Safilo makes eyeglasses and sunglasses for Polo Ralph Lauren and
many other of the top eyewear labels. Safilo also makes a full line of "house"
eyeglasses models, under several names like "Elasta" and others.
