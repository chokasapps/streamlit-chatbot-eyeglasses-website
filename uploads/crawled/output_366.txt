#  Oakley Eyeglasses

Oakley eyeglasses offer the perfect blend of stylish durability and
fashionable quality. Initially producing  polarized sunglasses  for mountain
bike-riders and rugged outdoorsmen, Oakley prescription glasses now include an
incredible collection of custom  Oakley single vision prescription lenses  or
Oakley Progressive lenses  for all Rxs and activities, although any lens can
be used in Oakley eyeglass frames. As an innovative designer of  glasses
technology, it currently holds more than 600 patents for Oakley glasses
frames, eyewear, and related products. As a company, all sunglasses,
eyeglasses, and other eyewear strives to be the best in the industry and
continually push ideas and technology to new levels.
