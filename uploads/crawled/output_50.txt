#  Alexander Daas Sunglasses

Alexander Daas sunglasses are designed to bring back optical principles
developed during the age of craftsmanship. Each sunglass frame is created with
thought and intention, made for a real face and fashioned by hand. The
collection focuses on petite faces, so it fills a void in the sunglasses
market. Women with small faces and small pupillary distances can now feel at
home browing a collection designed specifically for them. Whether it is a
sunglass frame or and optical frame, you can be sure that Alexander Daas
eyeglasses will cover you with fashion.
