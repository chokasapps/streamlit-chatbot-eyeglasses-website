#  Emporio Armani Glasses

Emporio Armani glasses are a fantastically crafted, high quality, design
orientated glasses collection featuring many different eyewear shapes and
styles. Ranging from thick, smooth and sleek frames, to classic thin metal
frames and everything in between, there is a large variety to choose from.
They are available in just about every modern shape - oval, round,
rectangular, aviator, cat eye and numerous others. The Armani glasses line is
divided into three brands,  Giorgio Armani glasses  ,  Emporio Armani  , and
Armani Exchange glasses  . Emporio Armani glasses and  Emporio Armani ****
sunglasses  can all be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just like
all of our  glasses  . The color choices that complement these shapes are just
as numerous, from professional blacks and greys to more vibrant and bright
casual colors. All three  eyewear  collections have a distinctive style and
price point, but the all share the Armani style ethic.
