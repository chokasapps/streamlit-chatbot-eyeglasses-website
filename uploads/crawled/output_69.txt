the model numbers: Easyclip sunglasses begin with EC, Takumi sunglasses with
TK, Easytwist sunglasses with ET. Easyclip sunglasses are known for their
magnetic clip on sunglasses; Takumi sunglasses are known for the style and
titanium frames; and Easytwist sunglasses are known for their incredible
ability to be twisted in any direction. Aspex sunglasses are available in a
variety of shapes, sizes, and colors.  Aspex Eyewear glasses  and Aspex
