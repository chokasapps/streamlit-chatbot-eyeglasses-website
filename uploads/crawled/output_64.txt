#  Armani Exchange Sunglasses

Armani Exchange sunglasses is a label created in 1991 by Giorgio Armani
designed to capture the spirit of youth, through the lens of urban, fashion
forward, young men and young women. sunglasses styles are created to feature
the characteristic strength, conceptual execution and daring use of color and
materials which is characteristic of the Armani name. Character and
intelligence are hallmarks of  Armani Exchange eyewear  , and, similar to the
clothing lines, the sunglasses collection also pushes the fashion envelope of
fashion. All  Armani Exchange glasses  and most Armani Exchange sunglasses can
be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just like all of our  sunglasses
. The Armani sunglasses line is divided into three brands,  Giorgio Armani
sunglasses  ,  Emporio Armani sunglasses  , and Armani Exchange. All three
eyewear  collections has a distinctive style and price point, but the all
share the Armani style ethic.
