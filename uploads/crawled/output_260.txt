#  JOE Eyeglasses

sophisticated, enlightened design alternative to traditional lifestyle brands,
existing to reflect and express the confidence of its discerning core
customer. It is the evocation of today's modern life, where success is earned
and style and quality come first from within.

  

Relevant and confident, Joseph Abboud JOE customers are true aficionados of
all that they value and live full lives at work and at play. Today's man lives
a life of achievement and substance; and to him,Joseph Abboud provides the
wardrobe, accessories (like JOE glasses) and home furnishings he needs to
approach his life with confidence and style.
