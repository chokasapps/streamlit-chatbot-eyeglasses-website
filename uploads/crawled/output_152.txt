#  Derek Lam Glasses

Derek Lam glasses exude the design and fashion ethic of Derek Lam  himself  .
Glasses frames carry the calculated simplicity and perfect detailing that you
can see in Derek Lam garments and accessories. Glasses styling is affected by
contemporary culture, in concert with modern art, architecture, and design
tastes. It would be impossible for Derek Lam to not be influenced by his New
York City home. Yet that influence shows itself in a reckless disregard for
bells and whistles, and instead focuses on simplicity. This simplicity is no
more evident that it is in the  Derek Lam eyewear  collection. All Derek Lam
glasses and most  Derek Lam sunglasses  can be customized with  **prescription
lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
