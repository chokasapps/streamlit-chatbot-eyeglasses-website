#  Kate Young Eyeglasses

Stylist Kate Young is one of the most important designers of our time, and her
new Kate Young Eyeglasses collection shows the importance of her design
esthetic. With one of the most varied and respected resumes in fashion today,
Kate is sought after by Hollywood celebrities for Oscar night, top
publications for cover shoots, and mega brands for advertising campaigns.

  

Kate began at Vogue Magazine, was quickly promoted in the fashion department
where she became the sittings editor for the “People Are Talking About…”
section. She then moved on to style the first-ever cover of Teen Vogue.
