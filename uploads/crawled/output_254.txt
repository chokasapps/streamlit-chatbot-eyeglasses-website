#  Jessica McClintock Glasses

Jessica McClintock glasses are the invention of the Jessica McClintock  brand
, which has carefully built a worldwide designer reputation for stylish
quality and fashionable craftsmanship. The popular glasses collection takes
advantage of its heritage with some classic frame styles but it also winks at
a new contemporary dynamism. Jessica McClintock glasses are the ideal choice
for men or women with a self-confident attitude who know what they want.
Jessica McClintock glasses and  Jessica McClintock sunglasses  can all be
customized with  **prescription lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  .
