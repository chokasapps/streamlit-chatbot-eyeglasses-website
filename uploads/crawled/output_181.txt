#  ECO Sunglasses

ECO sunglasses provide stylish prescription frames that are environmentally
friendly. The company is the first and only brand that has produced an optical
and sunglasses collection made with 95% recycled materials. All ECO biobased
sunglasses frames are constructed with a functional yet invisible spring
hinge, allowing for a more comfortable, lightweight fit. The temple tips are
adjustable, with a stainless steel core and anti-slip finish. All  ECO glasses
and most ECO sunglasses can be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just
like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
