#  Bottega Veneta Sunglasses

Bottega Veneta sunglasses are the next brand extension of the famous Italian
Atelier. The sunglasses collection is designed by the creative director, Tomas
Maier, who has been the driving force for over 15 years. Steeped in the
traditions of Italian master craftsmen, the house's  eyewear  artisans create
with an extreme attention to detail, high precision and relentless
perfectionism. And they have passed this attention to perfection through to
the sunglasses collection. All  Bottega Veneta glasses  and  Bottega Veneta
sunglasses  can be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just like all of
our  sunglasses  .
