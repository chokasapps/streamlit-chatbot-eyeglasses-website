#  Kenneth Cole Reaction Glasses

Kenneth Cole Reaction glasses reflect Kenneth Cole's modern, urban design
heritage with a youthful twist. The glasses collection shows a huge breadth of
brilliant colors and sporty shapes, and takes its inspiration from the the
downtime we spend involved in the urban environment. The  Kenneth Cole
Reaction eyewear  collection is trendy, comfortable, sporty and modern.
Glasses frames are designed to include smaller sizing with trendy,
fashionable, and popular style as well as the fashion-friendly use of bold
color pops. The popular glasses collection takes advantage of its heritage
with some classic frame styles but it also winks at a new contemporary
dynamism. Kenneth Cole Reaction glasses are the ideal choice for men or women
with a self-confident attitude who know what they want. All Kenneth Cole
Reaction glasses and can be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just
like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
