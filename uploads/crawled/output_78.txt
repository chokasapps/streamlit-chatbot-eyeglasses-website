#  Banana Republic Glasses

Banana Republic glasses frames all feature the same sleek, casual but elegant
style and fashion you would expect from all other Banana Republic products.
Banana Republic glasses are designed using the same stylish aesthetic you
would expect to see in any fashionable high-end retail store, they are made
with simple clean lines, strict attention to all details, soft, muted color
patterns to choose from and also a genuine collection “feel.” There are some
people who are just Banana Republic people. If you are one of those people,
then Banana Republic's eyeglasses are tailor made just for you. The popular
designer  **glasses** collection takes advantage of its heritage with some
classic frame styles but it also winks at a new contemporary dynamism. Banana
Republic glasses are the ideal choice for men or women with a self-confident
attitude who know what they want. Banana Republic glasses and  Banana Republic
**sunglasses** can all be customized with  **prescription lenses** . The
glasses frames are made with thin, lightweight metal or plastic and are
available in a variety of different stylish color patterns to choose from
along with a variety of different hinge designs, rim designs, and temple
designs.
