#  Liberty Sport Glasses

Liberty Sport glasses protective eyewear has been in production by the Liberty
Sport company since 1929. Liberty Sport glasses are designed to handle the
rigors of sport - unbreakable, tight fitting - and thus perfect for any PPE
application. Liberty sport glasses accomodate most kinds of  prescription
lenses  , to be used in hospitals, and sporting activities such as baseball,
softball, basketball, squash, and more. All Liberty Sport glasses and  Liberty
Sport sunglasses  can be customized with **prescription lenses** , just like
all of our  glasses  .
