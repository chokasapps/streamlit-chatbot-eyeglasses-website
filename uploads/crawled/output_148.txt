has carefully built a worldwide designer reputation for stylish quality and
fashionable craftsmanship. The popular eyeglasses collection features retro-
influence design and offers timeless styling, while taking advantage of its
heritage with some classic frame styles but it also winks at a new
contemporary dynamism. David Beckham glasses are the ideal choice for men with
a self-confident attitude who know what they want. All David Beckham glasses
and most  David Beckham sunglasses  can be customized with  **prescription
lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
