#  Argyleculture Glasses

Argyleculture glasses are the invention of  Russell Simmons  , an icon in
business, music, and fashion. Co-founder of Def Jam records, inventor of the
Phat Farm brand, Russell Simmons has a proven record of success and fashion
sense. Through his brand Argyleculture glasses he has carefully built a
worldwide designer reputation for stylish quality and fashionable
craftsmanship. The popular glasses collection takes advantage of its heritage
with some classic frame styles but it also winks at a new contemporary
dynamism. Argyleculture glasses are the ideal choice for men with a self-
confident attitude who know what they want. All Argyleculture glasses can be
customized with  **prescription lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  and
sunglasses  .
