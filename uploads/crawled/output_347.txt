#  Nautica Sunglasses

Nautica sunglasses are made by the  brand  Nautica, which is synonymous with
exceptional textiles, design, and fabrication—a true icon in menswear and now
in sunglasses. Sunglasses are fully polarized, combining style and function
with solid UV protection, in an array of timeless styles. The Nautica
sunglasses collections awaken the Nautica woman or man to a wardrobe full of
color and innovative fabrics. The feel is casual with a subtle flair of
sophistication. The Nautica sunglasses collection complements this style with
characteristic agility and subtlety. Inspired by water and a seafaring
heritage, the Nautica eyewear collection is for those who desire classic
styling with modern appeal. Crafted for comfort with a superior fit, each
frame has nautical elements incorporated into its design, with updated
colorations, materials and hardware for a distinctive look. All  Nautica
glasses  and most Nautica sunglasses can be customized with  **prescription
lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
