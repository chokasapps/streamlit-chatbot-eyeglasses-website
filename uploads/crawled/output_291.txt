#  Leon Max Eyeglasses

Leon Max eyewear is the creative brainchild of the designer with the same
name. Born Leonid Maksovich Rodovinsky, Leon Max was born Russian but
emigrated early when his parent sought political asylum. He worked at Tahari
in New York, and then founded Max Studio in 1979. His creative vision has
resulted in a very successful fashion house, 46 retail stores, and a well-know
design aesthetic which is shown in the Leon Max eyeglasses collection.
