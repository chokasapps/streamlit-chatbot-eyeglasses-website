#  Moschino Glasses

Moschino glasses are the invention of the Moschino brand, which has carefully
built a worldwide designer reputation for stylish quality and fashionable
craftsmanship. Founded in 1983 by  Franco Moschino  in  Milan  , Italy, the
popular glasses collection takes advantage of its heritage with some classic
frame styles but it also winks at a new contemporary dynamism. Moschino
glasses are the ideal choice for men or women with a self-confident attitude
who know what they want. Moschino glasses and  Moschino **** sunglasses  can
all be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just like all of our
glasses  .
