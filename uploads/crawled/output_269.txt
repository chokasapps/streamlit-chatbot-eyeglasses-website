#  Juicy Couture Glasses

Juicy Couture glasses are a fabulous range of women’s glasses that stand out
with incredible bold design, vibrant and striking color combinations and out
of the ordinary patterning. Bright fuchsia and cyan are the most eye catching
examples of this head turning  glasses  brand which sees influences from the
other products available in the rest of their ranges. Juicy Couture glasses
and  Juicy Couture sunglasses  can all be customized with  prescription lenses
.  With aviators and rounded rectangle lenses taking up most of the
collection, you can see that there is a definite theme running throughout
everything available, which shows how confident in their style choices Juicy
Couture are. They even offer a large range of sunglasses to make sure that
during the summer season, you can continue your stylish look that only Juicy
can offer. Metal and plastic are the two materials of choice, each chosen to
suit the particular style of frame, making sure that the most is made out of
