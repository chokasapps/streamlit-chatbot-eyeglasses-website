#  Dolce and Gabbana Sunglasses

Dolce and Gabbana sunglasses are designed for luxury and sophistication with
their sleek frames and statement-making lenses. You can’t deny that these
glasses are designed with style in mind. You’ll see them on the faces of top
models worldwide as they walk the runways. You’ll find advertisements in
magazine spreads for a true fashion icon. Out of all the glasses brands, Dolce
Gabbana sunglasses attract celebrities from around the world who have been
seen adorning some Dolce sunglasses on various occasions. So much is the craze
for these sunglasses that in 2010,  Madonna  , the renowned singer, took the
initiative to  design sunglasses for the label  .
