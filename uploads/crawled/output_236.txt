#  Hello Kitty Glasses

Hello Kitty glasses are official glasses frames licensed from  Sanrio  , the
Japanese company behind the Hello Kitty brand. Hello Kitty glasses are perfect
for kids and adults who are  kids  at heart. All Hello Kitty glasses frames
have the famous Hello Kitty logo on the temples so that wherever you are, your
Kitty will be with you! Each Hello Kitty  eyewear  frame features Hello Kitty
branding and logo treatments and is equipped with spring hinges. Colors range
from blue, brown, honey, lilac and rose to combinations of black and green and
black and pink. All Hello Kitty glasses can be customized with  **prescription
lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
