#  Alexander McQueen Sunglasses

Alexander McQueen sunglasses bear the name and glasses design aesthetic of the
legendary Lee Alexander McQueen, one of the most  visionary designers  of his
generation. Alexander McQueen has carefully built a worldwide designer
reputation for stylish quality and fashionable craftsmanship. The popular
sunglasses collection takes advantage of his heritage with some classic frame
styles but it also winks at a new contemporary dynamism. Alexander McQueen
sunglasses are the ideal choice for men or women with a self-confident
attitude who know what they want. All  Alexander McQueen glasses  and most
Alexander McQueen sunglasses can be customized with  **prescription lenses** ,
just like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
