#  Marc Jacobs Glasses

Marc Jacobs glasses are designed by the highly regarded creative director for
the fashion house of Louis Vuitton. Targeted to women, the Marc Jacobs glasses
collection is a celebration of vintage  glasses  designs paired with urban
sensibility. Included are 12 styles-eight plastics and four metals. Shapes
consist of a predominance of rectangular designs in traditional, soft and
shallow silhouettes as well as a selection of modified ovals. Marc Jacobs
glasses and  Marc Jacobs sunglasses  can all be customized with  prescription
lenses  . Colors range from chocolate brown, bronze, burgundy, shiny wine and
gold in the metals to combinations of black/havana, tortoise/ivory,
navy/crystal, plum/caramel and violet/yellow/plum in the plastics. Signature
MARC by Marc Jacobs details- jumbled and laser-engraved logos-accent the
eyewear. The Marc Jacobs glasses collection is an extremely wearable line that
is moderately priced, which enables just about anyone to have access to his
brilliant and creative designs.
