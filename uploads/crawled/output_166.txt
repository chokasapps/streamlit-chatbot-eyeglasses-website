#  Dolomiti Eyeglasses

In the eastern Alps of Italy, near the Austrian border and a short drive from
Venice, the Dolomiti mountains spring from the ground. In the valleys live
artisans who for over one hundred years have been crafting fine eyewear by
hand. Steeped in the Venetian decorative tradition, and the Northern Italian
instinct for fine quality, we work directly with the same factories making top
designer brands, to bring you this same craftsmanship **at half the price** .
And we have assembled a  Vintage Collection  as well.
