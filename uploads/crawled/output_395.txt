span of time, and created an array of designer  prescription sunglasses
exclusively designed for the ardent fashion lovers. Prada sunglasses have
their origin in Italy - the land of elite couture and the fashionista. Being
one of the most popular brands in sunglasses, Prada sunglasses span a range
that affords you the comfort as well as the exclusivity in style. The  Prada
range of sunglasses for women and sunglasses for men is so popular that it
