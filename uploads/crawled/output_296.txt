#  Lilly Pulitzer Glasses

Lilly Pulitzer glasses frames are popular for their stylish color. The glasses
come with a full rim, half rim, a skull temple, a universal temple along with
a variety of the trendiest and most fashionable shapes to choose from. The
popular  **glasses** collection takes advantage of its heritage with some
classic frame styles but it also winks at a new contemporary dynamism. Lilly
Pulitzer glasses are the ideal choice for men or women with a self-confident
attitude who know what they want. Lilly Pulitzer glasses and  Lilly Pulitzer
**sunglasses** can all be customized with  **prescription lenses** . They
feature the brightest colors, the most exciting designs, a warm and summery
fashion and these eyeglasses are a staple of style and fashion in trendy Palm
Beach Florida as they are almost considered part of the town uniform there for
picnics, exclusive parties, and any other occasion in Palm Beach.
