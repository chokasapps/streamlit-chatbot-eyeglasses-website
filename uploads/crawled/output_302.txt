#  Longchamp Sunglasses

Longchamp sunglasses are the invention of the Longchamp brand, which has
carefully built a worldwide designer reputation for stylish quality and
fashionable craftsmanship. The popular  **sunglasses** collection takes
advantage of its heritage with some classic frame styles but it also winks at
a new contemporary dynamism. Longchamp sunglasses are the ideal choice for men
or women with a self-confident attitude who know what they want.  Longchamp
glasses  and Longchamp **sunglasses** can all be customized with
**prescription lenses** . Infused with Parisian style and sophisticated
allure, Longchamp’s new eyewear collection stands out for its unique mix of
exclusive textures, iconic details and singularly feminine shapes. The new
selection is enriched with timeless styles and bold, geometric proportions
which masterfully blend elegance, ease and distinction.
