#  LAMB Sunglasses

LAMB sunglasses, by Gwen Stefani, are finally released! Gwen has been telling
the press that creating a sunglasses line is something that she has wanted to
do for a while. She is excited by the fashion opportunity that sunglasses
holds and she wants to design multiple collections. LAMB sunglasses are the
invention of  Gwen Stefani  , who has carefully built a worldwide designer
reputation for stylish quality and fashionable craftsmanship. The popular
sunglasses collection takes advantage of its heritage with some classic frame
styles but it also winks at a new contemporary dynamism. LAMB sunglasses are
the ideal choice for women with a self-confident attitude who know what they
want.  LAMB glasses  and LAMB **** sunglasses can all be customized with
**prescription lenses** , just like all of our  sunglasses  .
