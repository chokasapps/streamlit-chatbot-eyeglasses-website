exceptional textiles, design, and fabrication—a true icon in womenswear and
now in glasses. The Nine West glasses collections awaken women to a wardrobe
full of color and innovative fabrics. The feel is casual with a subtle flair
of sophistication. The Nine West eyewear collection complements this style
with characteristic agility and subtlety. All Nine West eyeglass frames on our
site may be customized with  prescription lenses  .
