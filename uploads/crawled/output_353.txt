#  Nicole Miller Glasses

The Nicole Miller glasses collection is composed mostly of acetate frames,
with larger eye sizes and many with a hint - or more than a hint - of cat eye.
Temples often feature inlaid decorations in pearl or silver, embossed or with
carvings, or some other kind of sculptural decoration. The color palette for
the glasses collection is muted, giving a professional air to the collection,
and relying on the decoration and shape for its femininity. Some models, like
the model Greene feature more vibrant tortoise colors. The popular
**glasses** collection takes advantage of its heritage with some classic frame
styles but it also winks at a new contemporary dynamism. Nicole Miller glasses
are the ideal choice for women with a self-confident attitude who know what
they want. Nicole Miller glasses and  Nicole Miller **sunglasses** can all be
customized with  **prescription lenses** . ...  Read More
