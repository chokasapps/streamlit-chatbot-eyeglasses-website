#  ECO Glasses

ECO glasses provide stylish prescription frames that are environmentally
friendly. The company is the first and only brand that has produced a glasses
and sunglasses collection made with 95% recycled materials. All ECO biobased
glasses frames are constructed with a functional yet invisible spring hinge,
allowing for a more comfortable, lightweight fit. The temple tips are
adjustable, with a stainless steel core and anti-slip finish. All ECO glasses
and most  ECO sunglasses  can be customized with  **prescription lenses** ,
just like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
