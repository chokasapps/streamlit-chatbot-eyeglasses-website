#  Lafont Glasses

Lafont glasses, in the  eyewear  industry, is a brand that evokes not only the
images of romanticism, but also the classical mystique behind love,
architecture and modern vintage styles. For those who have seen Lafont glasses
firsthand, adjectives will be shortsighted when expressing the sophistication
that this brand offers to customers. Every pair of  Lafont eyewear  shares
talented craftsmanship, style, and the spirit of Paris. Lafont glasses offer a
vast range of options for customers, from classical stylish looks to
outlandish and vintage options - all in the Reedition Collection. All Lafont
glasses and most  Lafont sunglasses  can be customized with  **prescription
lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
