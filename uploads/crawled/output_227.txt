#  Gucci Sunglasses

The Gucci collection of  sunglasses  was launched again in 2017, and since
then, they have redesigned a lot of their offerings. Gucci sunglasses have
often been referred to as some of the most fashionable accessories to ever
grace the market, not to mention that they are available in options suited to
men  and  women  . Gucci have a 90-year heritage and they also have a range of
prescription glasses  and oversized shades. You can expect to find a huge
range of unique styles and the new collection is made by the owner of Gucci,
meaning that the line-up is not made by a licensee anymore. This has helped
their new collection to really take off, and it has also helped to solidify
Gucci as being one of the most popular brands on the market. Gucci eyeglasses
is the ideal choice for both  men  and  women  everywhere with a self-
confident attitude who know what they want.  Gucci glasses  and Gucci
**sunglasses** can all be customized with  **prescription lenses** .
