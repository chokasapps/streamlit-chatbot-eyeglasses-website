#  Adensco Eyeglasses

Safilo Adensco Eyewear is one of the world's largest makers of eyeglasses and
sunglasses. Safilo makes glasses frames and sunglasses for Polo Ralph Lauren
and many other top eyewear labels. Safilo also makes a full line of "house"
eyeglasses models, under several names like "Adensco" and others. Made by a
leading Italian manufacturer Safilo, Adensco eyeglasses have remarkable
durability and elegance. Flexible in its design and innovative, Adensco
glasses provides a broad range of styling from young at heart to full-grown.
