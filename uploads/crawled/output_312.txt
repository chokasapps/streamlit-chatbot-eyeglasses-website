typically intellectually adventurous, curious, and artistically open-minded.
He/She is also athletically inclined. You are equally comfortable riding a
snowboard as wearing six inch heels on Madison Avenue. Marc by Marc Jacobs
sunglasses do not create you, but they do augment your reality.
