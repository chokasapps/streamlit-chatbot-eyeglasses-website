#  Adidas Sport Eyeglasses

Wherever there is an athletic contest taking place under the sun, someone is
wearing Adidas eyeglasses.  Adidas  created and popularized so many items of
sportswear, and now brings equal attention to the design and quality to their
Adidas eyeglasses.

  

The mission of Adidas eyeglasses is to build eyewear for athletes. Adidas
glasses are created for and together with athletes, whose visions set the tone
for the eyewear. Adidas glasses aim to support athletes in any way that they
can. Thus vision, protection, fit and flexibility are the critical concepts
that Adidas looks to uphold.
