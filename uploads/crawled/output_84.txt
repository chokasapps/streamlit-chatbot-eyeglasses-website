#  Betsey Johnson Glasses

Betsey Johnson glasses are the creative product of the irreverent and crazy
Betsey Johnson herself. An American fashion designer best known for her
feminine and whimsical fashions, "B.J.'s" designs are considered "over the
top" and embellished. She also is known for doing a cartwheel at the end of
her fashion shows. Betsey Johnson glasses are the invention of the Betsey
Johnson brand, which has carefully built a worldwide designer reputation for
stylish quality and fashionable craftsmanship. The popular  **glasses**
collection takes advantage of its heritage with some classic frame styles but
it also winks at a new contemporary dynamism. Betsey Johnson glasses are the
ideal choice for women with a self-confident attitude who know what they want.
Betsey Johnson glasses and  Betsey Johnson **sunglasses** can all be
customized with  **prescription lenses** .
