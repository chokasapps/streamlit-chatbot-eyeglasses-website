#  Hugo Boss Sunglasses

  
Hugo Boss sunglasses are made by Hugo Boss, the brand which is synonymous with
exceptional textiles, design, and fabrication—a true icon in menswear and now
in sunglasses. The Hugo Boss sunglasses collections awaken the Hugo Boss  man
to a wardrobe full of color and innovative fabrics. The feel is casual with a
subtle flair of sophistication. This is the  "Hugo" eyewear collection  , and
is different from the  "Boss" eyewear  collection, both of which are made by
Hugo Boss. The Hugo Boss sunwear collection complements this style with
characteristic agility and subtlety. Most  Hugo Boss glasses  and Hugo Boss
sunglasses can be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just like all of
our  sunglasses  .
