#  Aspire Glasses

Aspire glasses are the invention of the  Clearvision Eyewear Company  , an
American eyewear company founded in 1949, which has carefully built a
worldwide designer reputation for stylish quality and fashionable
craftsmanship. The popular Aspire glasses collection takes advantage of
Clearvision's heritage with some classic frame styles but it also winks at a
new contemporary dynamism.  Aspire eyewear  incorporates advanced materials
technology together with colorful and functional details in a collection for
men or women Aspire glasses frames provide a "barely there" feeling, which is
rare in the eyewear industry. All Aspire glasses and most  Aspire sunglasses
can be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just like all of our
