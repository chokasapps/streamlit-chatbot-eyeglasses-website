#  Kay Unger Glasses

Kay Unger glasses are representative of the highest fashion and style for
eyewear. Each model of the Kay Unger glasses  family  is designed, decorated,
and embellished with Swarovski crystals, metal work, and other high fashion
designs. The frames of this  eyewear  family are absolutely elegant,
completely unique, and are made with a unique and special interplay
combination of colors, designs, and decorations. Kay Unger glasses have been a
sophisticated fashion brand interpreting classic styles since 1995. Kay Unger
glasses are designed to both flatter women and also perfectly fit them. They
are also favorites among notable celebrities like  Oprah  ,  Paris Hilton  ,
Selma Hayek  , and many others. All Kay Unger glasses can be customized with
**prescription lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
