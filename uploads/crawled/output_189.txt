#  Ellen Tracy Glasses

Ellen Tracy glasses are designed by the company that was founded in 1949, and
since then, Ellen Tracy has evolved into a full women's lifestyle  brand  ,
designing quality apparel and glasses for dynamic, confident women. Today,
Ellen Tracy celebrates hyphenated women who gracefully multitask and do so
much for so many. Fusing classic style and contemporary innovation, Ellen
Tracy eyeglasses give today's women stylish confidence and fashionable flair
for their work in the world, from the office to the playground. Ellen Tracy
glasses and  Ellen Tracy **** sunglasses  can all be customized with
**prescription lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  .
