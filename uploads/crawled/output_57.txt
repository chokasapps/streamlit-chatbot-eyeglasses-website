carefully built a worldwide designer reputation for stylish quality and

its heritage with some classic frame styles but it also winks at a new
contemporary dynamism. Anne Klein glasses are is the ideal choice for men or
women with a self-confident attitude who know what they want. All Anne Klein
glasses and most  Anne Klein sunglasses  can be customized with
