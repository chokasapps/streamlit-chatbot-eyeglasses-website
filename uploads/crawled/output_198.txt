#  Ermenegildo Zegna Glasses

Ermenegildo Zegna glasses frames are available in metal, plastic, and
combinations of metal and plastic with an attention to details that the
Italian eyewear manufacturer, one of the world’s leading optics companies, is
known for. Ermenegildo Zegna glasses are the invention of the Ermenegildo
Zegna brand, which has carefully built a worldwide designer reputation for
stylish quality and fashionable craftsmanship. The popular  **glasses**
collection takes advantage of its heritage with some classic frame styles but
it also winks at a new contemporary dynamism. Ermenegildo Zegna glasses are
the ideal choice for men or women with a self-confident attitude who know what
they want. Ermenegildo Zegna glasses and  Ermenegildo Zegna **sunglasses** can
all be customized with  **prescription lenses** .
