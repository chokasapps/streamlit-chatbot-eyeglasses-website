#  Aristar Glasses

Aristar glasses are the invention of the Charmant Eyewear, one of the world's
largest and most fashionable manufacturers.  Charmant  has carefully built a
worldwide designer reputation for stylish quality and fashionable
craftsmanship. The popular Aristar glasses collection takes advantage of its
heritage with some classic frame styles but it also winks at a new
contemporary dynamism. Aristar glasses are the ideal choice for men or women
with a self-confident attitude who know what they want. Responding to a demand
for well-priced accessorising,  Aristar eyewear  designers took to the drawing
board to create a timeless look that would appeal to many tastes while still
providing excellent functionality. The result is Aristar eyewear: classically
styled frames for women and men who place a high value on reliable quality and
affordability. All  Aristar glasses  can be customized with  **prescription
lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
