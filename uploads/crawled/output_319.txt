#  Maui Jim Sunglasses

  

Maui Jim sunglasses are created to follow popular contemporary trends in
fashionable  sunglasses  , and they are engineered to balance aesthetics with
fit and classic stylish integrity. Along the way, Maui Jim sunglasses exhibit
new ways to combine advanced construction techniques with new frame materials,
which add up to the lightest and most comfortable designer frames possible.
Maui Jim sunglasses show the finest design details in their engineering and
manufacturing, resulting in a premium, functional, comfortable and elegant
pair of sunglasses. All Maui Jim prescription sunglasses are available with
prescription lenses  at our  Westport, CT  store.
