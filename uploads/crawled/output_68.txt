#  Aspex Glasses Brands: Easyclip, Takumi, and Easytwist Glasses

Aspex Eyewear is a high quality maker of some of the world's most innovative,
and patented, glasses brands. You can find them on our site by looking at the
model numbers: Easyclip glasses begin with EC, Takumi glasses with TK,
Easytwist glasses with ET. Easyclip glasses are known for their magnetic clip
on sunglasses; Takumi glasses are known for the style and titanium frames; and
Easytwist glasses are known for their incredible ability to be twisted in any
direction. Aspex glasses are available in a variety of shapes, sizes, and
colors. Aspex Eyewear glasses and  Aspex Eyewear sunglasses  can all be
customized with  **prescription lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  .
