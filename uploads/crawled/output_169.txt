#  Dolomiti Vintage Sunglasses

"Dolomiti VIntage" is a collection of new, never used, perfect condition
sunglasses made in the 1960's to 1990's. All sunglasses are carefully selected
for their quality components, styling, attention to detail, and were all hand
made in European factories, mostly Italy, Germany, or France. These sunglasses
we have procured from the "vault", archived products that have not seen the
light of day since they were made 40 to 60 years ago.

  

Nowadys, "classic" sunglasses steal design tips from these original  vintage
glasses,  and the new manufactured sunglasses are mostly made in China from
low quality components. Dolomiti VIntage sunglasses are the original, true
source - wouldn't you rather own the real McCoy versus a cheap knock off?
