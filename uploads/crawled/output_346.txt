#  Nautica Glasses

Nautica glasses are made by the  brand  Nautica, which is synonymous with
exceptional textiles, design, and fabrication—a true icon in menswear and now
in eyeglasses. The Nautica glasses collections awaken the Nautica woman or man
to a wardrobe full of color and innovative fabrics. The feel is casual with a
subtle flair of sophistication. The Nautica glasses collection complements
this style with characteristic agility and subtlety. Inspired by water and a
seafaring heritage, the Nautica eyewear collection is for those who desire
classic styling with modern appeal. Crafted for comfort with a superior fit,
each frame has nautical elements incorporated into its design, with updated
colorations, materials and hardware for a distinctive look. All Nautica
glasses and most  Nautica sunglasses  can be customized with  **prescription
lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
