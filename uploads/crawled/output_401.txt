#  Rag &amp; Bone Glasses

Ray &amp; Bone glasses are designed by the eponymous brand, which is a fusion of
British heritage and modern, New York design. Founded in New York in 2002, Rag
&amp; Bone has exploded into the the modern fashion scene, creating wearable
clothing, eyeglasses, and sunglasses that meld classic tailoring into an edgy,
Manhattan aesthetic. Rag &amp; Bone design is inspired by an attention to beauty
supported by top quality, skilled manufacturing.
