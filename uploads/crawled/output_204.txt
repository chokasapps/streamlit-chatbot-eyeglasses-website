#  Eye Care For Detroit Eyeglasses

We have partnered with Eye Care For Detroit (ECFD) to provide a diverse
collection of eyeglass frames for Detroit seniors at a very affordable price
point: $17 per frame. Founded in 1701, Detroit has a homebound community of
over 40,000 residents who have difficulty getting appropriate eyecare and
maintaining proper vision. They are at greater risk of avoidable and treatable
eye diseases because they lack affordable access to eye doctors and
eyeglasses. It is the mission of ECFD to provide access to high quality and
affordable eyecare to Detroit's homebound community, and Eyeglasses.com is
proud to sponsor the eyewear portion of this mission.
