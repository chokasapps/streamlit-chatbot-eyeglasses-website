#  Candies Sunglasses

Candies sunglasses are fun, perky, alert, alive and energetic express ideas
that describe the frames in Candie’s sunglasses collection. For the thoroughly
modern woman, these sunglasses contribute to her individual sense of style.
Candies sunglasses are casual enough to be perfect for poking around in blue
jeans, chic enough to wear at the board of directors meeting and while dining
by candlelight. The Candies sunglasses collection features a variety of lens
choices and beautiful styling. Candies sunglasses lenses are the heart of a
vision system especially designed for the modern woman those of us who insist
on seeing and seeing well. Candies sunglasses offer the ultimate in style,
versatility, and fit. The company is constantly trying to innovate to provide
better fit, fashion, and comfort.
