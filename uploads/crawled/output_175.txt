#  Ducks Unlimited Glasses

Ducks Unlimited glasses are produced by  the McGee Group  an independent,
family-owned American eyewear company. Ducks Unlimited glasses are a project
devoted to raising money for Ducks Unlimited, a non-profit organization
dedicated to wildlife preservation. Specifically, the mission of Ducks
Unlimited is to conserve, restore, and manage wetlands and similar habitats
for North American waterfoul ("ducks"). In its nearly 100 year history Ducks
Unlimited has conserved almost 13 milltion acres of land. The  Ducks Unlimited
eyewear  collection takes advantage of McGee's expertise to create a
collection of fashionable, classic glasses frame styles but it also winks at a
new contemporary dynamism. All Ducks Unlimited glasses and most  Ducks
Unlimited sunglasses  can be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just
like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
