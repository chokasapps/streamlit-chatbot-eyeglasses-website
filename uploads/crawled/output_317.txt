#  Marie Claire Glasses

  

Marie Claire glasses are more than just a decoration, and Marie Claire
Magazine is more than a pretty face. it is the fashion magazine with
character, substance, and depth, for women with a point of view, an opinion,
and a sense of humor. Marie Claire Magazine (and Marie Claire glasses) is a
culturally relevant experience that touches women beyond the newsstand. Marie
Claire understands that readers are more than any lavel or stereotype could
place on them, and Marie Claire celebrates that every reader is more than a
pretty face (especially if it is decorated with a pair of Marie Claire
eyeglasses).
