telling the press that creating an eyewear line is something that she has

glasses holds and she wants to design multiple collections. LAMB glasses are
the invention of  Gwen Stefani  , who has carefully built a worldwide designer
reputation for stylish quality and fashionable craftsmanship. The popular
glasses collection takes advantage of its heritage with some classic frame
styles but it also winks at a new contemporary dynamism. LAMB glasses are the
ideal choice for women with a self-confident attitude who know what they want.
