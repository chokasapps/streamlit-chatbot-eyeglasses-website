#  Carrera Glasses

Carrera glasses were born in 1956, in  Linz Austria  , the passion child of
Wilhelm Anger. Inspired by the  Carrera Panamericana  , the most dangerous
auto race in the world. Carrera glasses are known for their technical
expertise and craftsmanship, which is required to survive in such harsh
conditions. Carrera eyeglasses come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors.
Every Carrera eyeglasses frame is made to exacting standards, because the
designers know that your life absolutely depends on it. Carrera glasses and
Carrera **** sunglasses  can all be customized with ** prescription lenses  **
, just like all of our  glasses  . In 1964, Wilhelm Anger envisioned a
material that is lighter and harder than typical materials used in eyeglasses.
He patented it with the name, "Optyl", a heat-hardened plastic weighing less
than other materials, and also with a reduction of allergic reactions. Optyl
defines  Carrera eyewear  because of its improved fit stemming from a memory
effect that provides permanent elasticity and dimensional stability.
