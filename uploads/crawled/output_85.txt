#  Betsey Johnson Sunglasses

Betsey Johnson sunglasses emphasize personality, fun, free spirit,
celebration, happiness, bright color, movement, and contemporary modernity.
Every design depicts colors, textures, and prints that define Betsey's
creative vision season after season. The popular  sunglasses  collection takes
advantage of its heritage with some classic frame styles but it also winks at
a new contemporary dynamism. Betsey Johnson glasses are the ideal choice for
women with a self-confident attitude who know what they want.  Betsey Johnson
glasses  and Betsey Johnson **sunglasses** can all be customized with
**prescription lenses** .
