#  Invincilites Eyeglasses

INVINCILITES® by Zyloware offers rimless technology for the 21st century by
keeping the focus on the eyes, not the eyewear. Each style is designed using
lightweight materials appropriate for men and women of all ages.  
  
• BETA Collection is hypo-allergenic, 100% beta titanium. Unlike other rimless
beta titanium frames, Invincilites® BETA fold flat.  
  
• SIGMA Collection features customizable ultra-thin stainless steel metal
bridges and stainless steel endpieces for adjustability, with colorful
Grilamid® temples that fold flat.
