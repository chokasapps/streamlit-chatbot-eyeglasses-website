#  Capri Glasses

Capri glasses are the invention of the Capri Optics  company  , which has
carefully built a worldwide designer reputation for stylish quality and
fashionable craftsmanship at a reasonable price. The popular  Capril eyewear
collection takes advantage of its heritage with some classic frame styles but
it also winks at a new contemporary dynamism. Capri glasses are the ideal
choice for men or women with a self-confident attitude who know what they
want. All Capri glasses can be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just
like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
