#  Lafont Sunglasses

Lafont sunglasses, in the  eyewear  industry, is a brand that evokes not only
the images of romanticism, but also the classical mystique behind love,
architecture and modern vintage styles. For those who have seen Lafont
sunglasses firsthand, adjectives will be shortsighted when expressing the
sophistication that this brand offers to customers. Every pair of  Lafont
eyewear  shares talented craftsmanship, style, and the spirit of Paris. Lafont
sunglasses offer a vast range of options for customers, from classical stylish
looks to outlandish and vintage options - all in the Reedition Collection. All
Lafont glasses  and most Lafont sunglasses can be customized with
**prescription lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
