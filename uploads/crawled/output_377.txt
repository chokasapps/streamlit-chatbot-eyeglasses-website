#  Paradigm Sunglasses

Paradigm sunglasses are the invention of the Paradigm brand, which has
carefully built a worldwide designer reputation for stylish quality and
fashionable craftsmanship. The popular sunglasses collection takes advantage
of its heritage with some classic frame styles but it also winks at a new
contemporary dynamism. Paradigm sunglasses are the ideal choice for men or
women with a self-confident attitude who know what they want. All  Paradigm
glasses  and most Paradigm sunglasses can be customized with  **prescription
lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
