#  A&amp;A Optical Eyeglasses

A&amp;A Optical Company was founded in 1971 by Alex and Agnes Liener. In 37 years,
the company has developed and brought to the market 7 established eyewear
brands and over 300 glasses styles focused on market specific collections
covering all demographic segments. From unique, one of a kind glasses looks,
European inspired glasses designs to classic eyeglasses value styles, A&amp;A
Optical is dedicated to delivering to you the most innovative, premium
eyeglasses products and exceptional customer service to meet the growing
demands. A&amp;A Optical Eyewear continues to offer our partners the "Ultimate
eyewear buying experience".
