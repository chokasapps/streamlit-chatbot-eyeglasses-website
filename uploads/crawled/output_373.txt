#  Original Penguin Sunglasses

Original Penguin sunglasses stem from a tradition that goes back over 100
years to a Minnesota factory, where Abbot Pederson worked as a salesman for
Munsingwear. Adopting the cute and fashionable Penguin, Pederson's designs
became adopted and popularized by some the greatest celebrities of all time,
including  Frank Sinatra  ,  Dean Martin  ,  Arnold Palmer  ,  Clint Eastwood
and many more.
