#  Armani Exchange Glasses

Armani Exchange glasses is a label created in 1991 by Giorgio Armani designed
to capture the spirit of youth, through the lens of urban, fashion forward,
young men and young women. glasses styles are created to feature the
characteristic strength, conceptual execution and daring use of color and
materials which is characteristic of the Armani name. Character and
intelligence are hallmarks of  Armani Exchange eyewear  , and, similar to the
clothing lines, the glasses collection also pushes the fashion envelope of
fashion. All Armani Exchange glasses and most  Armani Exchange sunglasses  can
be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  .
The Armani glasses line is divided into three brands,  Giorgio Armani glasses
,  Emporio Armani  , and Armani Exchange. All three  eyewear  collections have
a distinctive style and price point, but the all share the Armani style ethic.
