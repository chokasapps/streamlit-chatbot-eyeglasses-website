#  Paradigm Glasses

Paradigm glasses are the invention of the Paradigm brand, which has carefully
built a worldwide designer reputation for stylish quality and fashionable
craftsmanship. The popular glasses collection takes advantage of its heritage
with some classic frame styles but it also winks at a new contemporary
dynamism. Paradigm glasses are the ideal choice for men or women with a self-
confident attitude who know what they want. All Paradigm glasses and most
Paradigm sunglasses  can be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just
like all of our  glasses  and  sunglasses  .
