#  Jones New York Glasses

Jones New York glasses are a collection of incredibly sleek looking glasses
frames, with beautiful lines - the perfect eyewear for the woman that values
high class fashion and stunning quality. Most of the sets of frames in the
range show a unique twist or novel feature that not only makes the glasses
stand out from other brands, but makes each frame stand out from others within
the collection. With interestingly styled arms, especially those with tactile
touches, hollowed out sections and non-traditional shapes and curves, the only
problem will be choosing just one of them to complete your look. To top off
this amazing selection, the majority of these frames also come with a hard
case included and manufacturer's warranty for that extra piece of mind.  Jones
New York glasses  can all be customized with  **prescription lenses** , just
like all of our  glasses  .
