#  Champion Sunglasses

Champion sunglasses are from that  brand  that we all know and love -
Champion. You are a Champion! Especially when you wear Champion  sunglasses
frames. This glasses collection is notable due to its flexibility - all the
frames use a flexible material. The glasses - and  Champion glasses  \- are
durable, well made, and specifically designed for larger people with larger
eye sizes and deeper lenses. Just add  prescription lenses  , and you are good
to go. The brand itself has been around a long time, and finds its roots back
in 1919, in Rochester New York. By the 1930s, the brand became known for
sweatshirts, tee shirts, and work socks. Early on, Champion was the leader is
sports jerseys, and, believe it or not, is the inventor of the hoodie! Not
only that, but Champion also invented the Jogbra, which, get this, was first
designed and constructed from two jock straps sewn together! Who new?
