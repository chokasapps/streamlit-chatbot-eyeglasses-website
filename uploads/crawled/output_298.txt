#  Line Art Glasses

Line Art glasses are jewelry; jewelry for the face, and frame for the eyes,
and the embodiment of beauty in comfort. Created by  Charmant Eyewear  , a
world leader in making fashionable eyewear, Line Art glasses are the artist's
creation of a glasses frame whose delicate lightness offers women a wearing
comfort that they have never before experienced. The popular  Line Art eyewear
collection takes advantage of its skill and experience to invent a new
contemporary dynamism. Line Art glasses are the ideal choice for women with a
self-confident attitude who know what they want. All Line Art glasses can be
customized with  **prescription lenses** , just like all of our  glasses  and
sunglasses  .
