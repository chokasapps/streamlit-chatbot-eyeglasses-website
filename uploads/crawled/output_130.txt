#  **Coach Sunglasses**

Coach sunglasses are not like other sunglasses brands. Coach makes sunglasses
offering inimitable style and high-quality materials, designed to protect your
eyes from the sun all day long. Coach Inc. was founded in 1941 and specializes
in small leather goods and accessories. It is best known for its hand bag,
which are beloved by women, and the bag concepts rely on beautiful shapes,
details, colors, and textures. Coach sunglasses are designed the same way, and
they all can be customized with  prescription lenses  , to make them into
prescription sunglasses  or  polarized sunglasses  .
