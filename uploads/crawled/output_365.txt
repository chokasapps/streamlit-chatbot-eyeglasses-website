#  Oakley Sunglasses

Oakley sunglasses deliver the perfect blend of stylish durability and
fashionable quality. Initially producing  polarized sunglasses  for mountain
bike-riders and rugged outdoorsmen, Oakley now includes an incredible
collection of custom  Oakley single vision prescription lenses  or  Oakley
Progressive lenses  for all prescriptions and activities, although any
prescription lenses can be used in an Oakley model. As an innovative designer
of  glasses  technology, it currently holds more than 600 patents for
sunglasses,  eyewear  , and related materials.  Oakley sunglasses for men  ,
Oakley women's sunglasses  , and  Oakley youth sunglasses  were all developed
specifically for the needs of those groups. As a company, all  Oakley  strives
to be the best in the industry and continually push ideas and technology to
new levels. That drive led the company to invent  Oakley Prizm lenses  , which
can be ordered standard or with a prescription.
