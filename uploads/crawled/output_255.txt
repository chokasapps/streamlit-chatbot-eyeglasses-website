#  Jhane Barnes Eyeglasses

Jhane Barnes glasses are created by Jhane Barnes. In her own words, "I'm
passionate about designing for men. I love creating beautiful and exciting
glasses using new materials and technologies. My eyeglasses frame materials
are unique because my team and I design all of them ourselves. I like making
guys look great in eyeglasses and tailored clothing. And I obsess over
details: buttons, cuffs, pockets, collars, the thread used for sewing-it's all
important. And the glasses fit has to be just right. I create my own glasses
patterns to make sure the lines fall just where they should. Rolling it all
together: I spend my time creating great men's fashions with novel fabrics. I
hope you enjoy wearing Jhane Barnes glasses as much as I enjoy creating them."
