#  Michael Kors Sunglasses

Michael Kors sunglasses were launched in 2004, along with the MICHAEL Michael
Kors and KORS Michael Kors lines launched, and they joined the original
Michael Kors collection label. Michael Kors prescription sunglasses were added
to fill out the collection of women's handbags, women's shoes and also ready-
to-wear womens apparel. New York, Beverly Hills, Palm Beach, Manhassett, and
Chicago are just some of the places Michael Kors sunglasses can be found. The
30th anniversary of Michael's business was celebrated in the year 2011 and he
has over 770 lifestyle stores globally. Michael is very popular for his jet
set sportswear style.  Michael Kors glasses  and Michael Kors **** sunglasses
