#  John Varvatos Sunglasses

John Varvatos is the bad boy of iconic American designers. John Varvatos
sunglasses carry this design ethic, in whch old world craftsmanship and
refined tailoring come crashing together with modern innovations and rock and
roll sensibility. Edgy, sensitive, carefully made - all John Varvatos products
carry that dichotomy of a generation coming into power that was weaned in
Woodstock and bad 1970's haircuts.

  

  
The John Varvatos sunglasses collection includes plastic and metal frames. The
plastic models have careful detailing in metalwork, with other design elements
that show how much we care about quality, even though we like to show a
carefree facade.
